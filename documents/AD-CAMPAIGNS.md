## Campaign Strategy Overview

Spotless Carwash is a single-location automatic + self-serve car wash in Forest Park, IL with one transactional offer surfaced online: pre-paid paper tokens redeemable at the automatic bays at a guaranteed wash credit ($8/$9/$10/$12 depending on color), sold in 4-packs at a flat ~$5 discount per pack. The realistic ad strategy here is **hyperlocal** — every dollar spent outside ~5 miles of the wash bay is wasted. Total budget recommendation is modest ($800–$1,500/month) with 50% on Google (high-intent local search owns this category) and 35% on Meta (geo-targeted feed/reels for prospecting and gift-buyer audiences).

Two parallel campaign tracks: **(1) Visit-driver** ads for nearby drivers searching "car wash near me" or seeing geo-targeted social ads — these route to the homepage / Google Business Profile / directions. **(2) Token-buyer** ads aimed at regulars and gift-buyers who'd pre-purchase a 4-pack — these route to the tokens page. The token campaign carries a built-in margin advantage: pre-paid revenue de-risks slow weather days and the $5/4-pack "discount" is fixed and predictable.

**Critical pre-launch blocker:** The token-purchase page is currently HTTP (not HTTPS) and not mobile-friendly (see SEO-AUDIT.md). Do not run paid traffic to it until that's fixed — Meta and Google will both throttle quality scores for non-secure landing pages, and mobile click-through-to-conversion will collapse on a 900px-fixed table layout. Visit-driver ads (which point to GBP / phone / directions) can launch immediately.

### Geo (all platforms)
- **Primary radius:** 3 miles from Forest Park, IL (covers Forest Park, Oak Park, River Forest, Maywood, Berwyn, north Cicero, far-west Chicago — Austin, Galewood)
- **Secondary radius:** 3–7 miles (Elmwood Park, North Riverside, LaGrange Park, Bellwood) — lower bid

- **Intent signals:** "near me" queries, navigational queries, "open now"
- **Device:** mobile +20% bid adjustment (most "car wash near me" searches happen from a car/phone)
- **Daypart:** weekends +30%, weekday evenings +15%, weather-driven (boost on first dry day after rain/snow)

## Testing Plan (Priority Order)

1. **Audience (Meta only):** 3mi vs 5mi radius — does the wider radius bleed budget or find new customers? Run 7 days, $100/variant.
2. **Offer framing (Meta token ads):** "$27 for 4 washes" vs "Save $5 per 4-pack" vs "Tokens never expire" — which hook converts? Same creative, three primary-text variants.
3. **Visual style (Meta):** Polished product photo vs. owner face-to-camera vs. POV through-the-wash reel — which gets the lowest CPM and highest CTR locally?
4. **Headline (Google Search):** Brand-led ("Spotless Carwash") vs. benefit-led ("Save $5 With Wash Tokens") vs. urgency ("Open Now") — let RSA optimization run 14 days then review.
5. **CTA (Meta token):** "Shop Now" vs. "Order Now" vs. "Get Yours."
6. **Landing page:** Once HTTPS + mobile fix ships, A/B the tokens page itself (one CTA per pack vs. all in one table; with vs. without testimonial section).

**Rules:**
- One variable per test
- Minimum 7 days or 1,000 impressions per variant
- Kill anything at 2× target CPA after 5 days
- Scale winners 20% per week, never 2× overnight
