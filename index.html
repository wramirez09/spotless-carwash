02 / How it works

Watch the lights.
Don't back up.

Our automatic bays run on a simple traffic-light system. Wait for green, pull forward slow, hit the stop signal, and let the timer count down.

01

Choose your wash

Cash or card at the bay station — Basic, Clean, Spotless, or Showroom.

02

Wait for green

Don't pull in unless you have a green light. Red & yellow mean wait.

03

Pull forward slowly

Stop when the front-left tire hits the red light. Then let go & sit tight.

04

Exit slooowly

Door opens, dryers blow. Pull through & check the timer overhead.
